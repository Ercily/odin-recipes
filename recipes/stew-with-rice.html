Stew with rice's recipe

This dish is one of the most popular dishes within the Cap Verdean community.

Peas, lentil, carrot or cabbage are the main vegetables, stewed with some meat and served with rice.

Very delicious!!

Recipe for 4 persons :

Ingredients

  • 800g of beans
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 cabbage
  • 500g of meat
  • Olive oil
  • Seasoning
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • 1/3 of garlic
    • Curry
    • Chicken stock cube
    • Herbs
  • 400g of rice

Steps

  1. Clean the beans, pour some water into a pot, then the beans and let cook during 1-2 hours.
  2. Prepare the mean in a pot: clean, season, add 2 tbsp of olive oil, mix the whole and let soak during 20-30min.
  3. Cook the meat until the meat is tender, generally 2 hours.
  4. Prepare the carrots and cabbage and cook it during 30min.
  5. When the meat is ready, pour some water, a little or salt or a chicken stock cube and the vegetables. Let simmer during 20-30min.
  6. To serve with rice and enjoy!
