Mulligatawny soup's recipe

This dish is one of the best soup you could have today.

Mulligatawny soup means "pepper water", and curry is the particular ingredient that gives this incredible dish such a delicious flavor.

The mere idea of it make your mouth water!

Recipe for 1 person :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup white rice
  • 1/2 apple, cored and chopped
  • 1 skinless, boneless chicken breast half-cut into cubes
  • 1 pinch dried thyme
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, heated

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add onions, celery and carrot and saut√© until soft, 5 to 7 minutes. Add flour and curry, and cook 5 more minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. Add chicken broth, mix well, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for about 30 minutes. Add apple, rice, chicken, thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer until rice is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Just before serving, stir in hot cream.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
